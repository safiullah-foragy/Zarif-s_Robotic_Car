## Color Coding for Wires (Recommended)

- **Red:** +5V Power
- **Black:** Ground (GND)
- **Yellow:** Signal/Data lines
- **Orange:** Motor connections
- **Blue:** Serial communication (TX/RX)
- **Green:** Sensor connections

## Critical Connections Checklist

- [ ] All grounds connected together (Arduino, ESP8266, L293D, Battery)
- [ ] L293D Enable pins (1 & 9) connected to 5V
- [ ] Motor power (VP) connected to battery positive
- [ ] ESP8266 and Arduino serial pins connected (TX to RX, RX to TX)
- [ ] Ultrasonic sensor VCC to 5V, GND to ground
- [ ] Servo connected to 5V, GND, and PWM signal pin
- [ ] All 4 motor inputs connected to Arduino digital pins
- [ ] Motors connected to L293D output pins

## Power Requirements

| Component | Voltage | Current |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Arduino UNO | 5V | 50mA |
| ESP8266 | 5V | 80-170mA |
| L293D IC | 5V logic | 20mA |
| Motors (2x) | 7.4-12V | 500mA each |
| Servo SG90 | 5V | 100-250mA |
| Ultrasonic | 5V | 15mA |

**Total 5V Current:** ~400mA (use 1A+ power supply)
**Total Motor Current:** ~1A (use appropriate battery)
